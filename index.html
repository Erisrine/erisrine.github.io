About me

I'm Ivan, an Italian technical sound designer.
I have three years of experience in the audio production industry. So far, I have worked with a plethora of creative individuals and groups, including animators, film-makers, game designers and mod makers, as well as event organisers. When I’m not making sound or building audio systems for games, I am usually speedrunning my favourite titles or playing through new ones, fuelling my ever growing and everlasting passion for video games.
